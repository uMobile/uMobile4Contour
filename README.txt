umobile4contour
===============

uMobile4Contour is a simple uMobile application extension to manage Contour entries from your smartphone.

To use it you must have uMobile app installed on your phone and uMobile package installed on your Umbraco instance.

For more information: http://our.umbraco.org/projects/backoffice-extensions/umobile

Once uMobile is working on your phone, just install uMobile4Contour package on Umbraco and play around.
